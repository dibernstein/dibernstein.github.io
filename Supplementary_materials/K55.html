Supplementary Materials for "K_{5,5} is fully reconstructable in C^3"

This webpage contains supplementary material for the paper K_{5,5} is fully reconstructable in C^3 by Daniel Irving Bernstein, and Steven J. Gortler 

Download and uncompress K55.zip and consult the README file. Running these computations requires the geng comand from the Nauty and Traces package, as well as Mathematica.
