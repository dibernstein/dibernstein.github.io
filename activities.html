Upcoming and ongoing activities -- Daniel Irving Bernstein

While the COVID-19 pandemic makes it unsafe for us to attend conferences and departmental seminars, I will be organizing a virtual seminar on algebraic matroids and rigidity theory. Please email me if you would like to be added to the mailing list, or if you would like to give a talk!

Along with Diego Cifuentes, I organize a seminar on Algebra, Statistics, and Optimization at MIT in IDSS. This is on hiatus during the COVID-19 pandemic, but will resume once it is safe to do so.

I will spend January through June of 2021 as a Jerrold E. Marsden Postdoctoral Fellow at the Fields Institute in Toronto during the Thematic Program on Geometric Constraint Systems, Framework Rigidity, and Distance Geometry.
